  • Greetings from TESDA Sorsogon National Agricultural School!

    Our school has been offering technical vocation education and training (TVET) programs which I believe is becoming more and more popular today. The demand for skilled workers is increasing and our school continue to develop competent workers to answer the need of local community and also of other countries

    As an agricultural institution, we advocate agriculture and its role in the social and economic growth of the community. We share our efforts to achieve food security and improve nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture through our programs.

    We expand access to TVET to all clients through our institution-based, community-based, scholarship programs, competency assessment and certification and industry immersion of students to industry partners and stakeholders.

    We are thankful for TESDA management for the continuing support to improvement of quality education and accessibility through the use of information technology.

    LEONOR B. DIAZ

    Vocation School Administrator III
